Our Services

Comprehensive Home-Based Therapy

We offer a full spectrum of rehabilitation services delivered by experienced, licensed professionals right in your home.

🏃

Physical Therapy (PT)

Restoring movement and function through evidence-based interventions tailored to each patient's unique needs and goals.

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Balance and fall prevention
  • Pain management
  • Mobility and strength training
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Geriatric physical therapy
✋

Occupational Therapy (OT)

Helping patients regain independence in daily activities through skilled therapeutic interventions and adaptive strategies.

  • Activities of daily living (ADL) training
  • Fine motor skill development
  • Cognitive rehabilitation
  • Home safety assessments
  • Adaptive equipment training
  • Energy conservation techniques
🗣

Speech-Language Pathology (SLP)

Addressing communication, cognitive-linguistic, and swallowing disorders to improve quality of life and safety.

  • Speech and language therapy
  • Swallowing (dysphagia) therapy
  • Cognitive-linguistic treatment
  • Voice therapy
  • Aphasia rehabilitation
  • Augmentative communication

Our Mission

At Synergy Therapy Services, we believe that healing happens best where you feel most at home. Our mission is to deliver exceptional, patient-centered rehabilitation services that empower individuals to achieve their highest level of independence and well-being.


We combine clinical excellence with genuine compassion, ensuring every patient receives the personalized attention they deserve.

Dedicated to Your Recovery & Well-Being

Synergy Therapy Services is a home health therapy provider serving patients throughout New Jersey. Our team of licensed and experienced therapists is dedicated to delivering high-quality, individualized care in the environment where patients are most comfortable — their own home.

We work collaboratively with physicians, caregivers, and families to develop comprehensive treatment plans that address each patient's unique needs and goals. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures seamless coordination between Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, and Speech-Language Pathology services.
